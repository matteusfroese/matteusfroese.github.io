This is the page for the Combinatorics and Topology Learning seminar run by me. We will broadly look at the intersection of combinatorics and topology. If this sounds crazy to you, just remember that "Algebraic Topology" used to be called "Combinatorial Topology". Each term will have a slightly different flair to it. You can find the details for the seminar each term below:

Summer 2026

This term we will study the Poset Topology by Michelle Wachs. You can find a link to this paper here.
The location and time are Mondays 2-3pm in MC 6029, starting May 25th.

  • Monday May 25: Matteus Froese, Introduction to Poset Topology.
  • Monday June 15: Matteus Froese, Top dimensional cohomology and the homology of shellable complexes.
  • Monday June 22: Guilherme Zeus Dantas e Moura, Introduction to Representation theory of the symmetric group.

Fall 2025

This term started a bit late, but we are studying the moduli space of graphs.
The location and time are Wednesdays 3:30-4:30pm in MC 6483.

  • Wednesday Nov 5: Matteus Froese, the definition of the Moduli Space of Graphs and some applications.
  • Wednesday Nov 12: Matteus Froese, Questions from last week, labelled graph representations, and possible future directions.
  • Wednesday Nov 19: Ty Ghaswala, The parallel story: Teichmuller spaces.
  • Wednesday Nov 26: Kledion Shahini, Computing the Euler Characteristic.
  • Wednesday Dec 3: Karen Yeats, The Moduli Space of Curves.
